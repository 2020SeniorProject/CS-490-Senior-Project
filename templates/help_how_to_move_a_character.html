How to Move a Character

This document details how to move a character's icon on the battlemap.

  1. You must already have a character or NPC added to the battlemap in order to move them (see: How to Add a character to the Battlemap).
  2. Hover over the character's icon on the battlemap.
  3. Click and drag the character icon to where you would like to place them. You cannot drag the icon outside of the battlemap.
  4. You have moved a character!
