How to Open a Room

This document details how to delete a room. You need to be logged in in order to have access to rooms.

  1. From the home screen, click the "Room" tab on the navigation bar at the top.
  2. You should see a list of all your rooms. Click the name of the room you want to open.
  3. You should a battle map with a form in the upper right corner. Click the green "Open room" button.
  4. You will be redirected to the link for your open room!
