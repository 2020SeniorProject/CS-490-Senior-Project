How to Join a Room

This document details how to join a room. You need to be logged in in order to join rooms.

  1. Note: You need a character in order to join a room. Follow this guide to create a character.
  2. Get the specific room id to the room you wish to join from your DM. It is an 8-character alphanumeric code located in the middle of the navigation bar in the room.
  3. From the home screen, enter the room id into the "Play Room ID" form and click the red "Choose room" button.
  4. You have now joined the room!
