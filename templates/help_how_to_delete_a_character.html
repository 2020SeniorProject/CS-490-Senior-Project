How to Delete a Character

This document details how to delete a character. You need to be logged in in order to have access to characters.

  1. From the home screen, click the "Characters" tab on the navigation bar at the top.
  2. You should see a table listing all of your character. Find the character you want to delete in your list.
  3. Click the red button with a trash can in the last column of the row.
  4. The character will is now deleted!
