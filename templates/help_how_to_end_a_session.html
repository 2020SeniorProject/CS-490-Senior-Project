How to End a Session

This document details how to end a session. You need to have opened a room as a Dungeon Master in order to end a session.

  1. Click the yellow button labeled "End Session!" under the header "DM's Tools".
  2. You will be asked via a pop-up if you are sure you want to end the session. Don't worry! The battlemap and all character locations will be saved. Click OK.
  3. You will be returned to home page.
  4. You have ended the session!
