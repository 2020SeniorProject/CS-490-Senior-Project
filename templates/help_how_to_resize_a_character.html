How to Resize a Character

This document details how to resize a character's icon on the battlemap.

  1. You must already have a character or NPC added to the battlemap in order to resize them (see: How to Add a character to the Battlemap).
  2. Hover over the character's icon on the battlemap.
  3. You will see a handle appear in the bottom right corner of the character icon.
  4. Click and drag the handle to resize the character.
  5. You have resized a character!
