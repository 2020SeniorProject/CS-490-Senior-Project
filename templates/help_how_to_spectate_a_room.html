How to Spectate a Room

This document details how to spectate a room.

  1. Get the specific room id to the room you wish to spectate from your DM. It is an 8-character alphanumeric code located in the middle of the navigation bar in the room.
  2. From the home screen, enter the room id into the "Spectate Room ID" form and click the red "Choose room a to spectate" button.
  3. You are now spectating the room!
