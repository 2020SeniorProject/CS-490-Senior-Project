How to Delete a Room

This document details how to delete a room. You need to be logged in in order to have access to rooms.

  1. From the home screen, click the "Room" tab on the navigation bar at the top.
  2. You should see a list of all your rooms. Find the room you want to delete in the list.
  3. Click the red "Delete" button associated with your room, and press "OK" in the popup.
  4. The room is now deleted!
