How to End a Charater's Turn

This document details how to end a character's turn. You must have previously added a character to the battlemap, a battle must be ongoing, and it must be the character's turn in order to end a character's turn.

  1. A battle must have been started by the Dungeon Master (see: How to Start a Battle), and you must already have added a character to the battlemap (see How to Add a character to the Battlemap).
  2. Unless you are the Dungeon Master, it must be your character's turn in order to end their turn.
  3. Click the red button in the bottom left corner of the battlemap screen labeled "End Turn!"
  4. You have ended the current character's turn!
