How to Update a Character's Initiative

This document details how to update a character's initiative. You must already have added a character to the battlemap in order to change their initiative.

  1. You must already have a character or NPC added to the battlemap in order to update their initiative (see: How to Add a character to the Battlemap).
  2. In the bottom left of the battlemap screen, you will see a dropdown listing your characters on the map. Select the character whose initiative you wish to update.
  3. Type the initiative you wish to change your character's value to in the input field labeled "Initiative".
  4. Click the blue buttom labeled "Update Initiative" below the dropdown and the initiative input field.
  5. Your character's initative has been updated!
