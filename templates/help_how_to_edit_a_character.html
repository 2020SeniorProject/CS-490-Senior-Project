How to Edit a Character

This document details how to edit a character. You need to be logged in in order to have access to characters.

  1. From the home screen, click the "Characters" tab on the navigation bar at the top.
  2. You should see a table listing all of your character. Find the character you want to edit in your list and click the yellow button with a pencil in the last column of that row.
  3. You should have a form titled "Edit a character". Change the values you want and click on "Change Character!. If your form has errors, fix the indicated issues, then resubmit."*
  4. Upon submitting, you should be brought back to the main character page. You will see your edited character in your list of characters!

*The form has the following rules

  • Name: Cannot be empty, maximum 25 characters, can only be made of alphabet characters and spaces.
  • Race: Cannot be empty.
  • Subrace: Cannot be empty.
  • Speed: Cannot be empty, must be a positive number equal to or less than 1000.
  • Class: Cannot be empty.
  • Subclass: Cannot be empty.
  • Level: Cannot be empty, must be a positive number equal to or less than 20.
  • Strength: Cannot be empty, must be a positive number equal to or less than 30.
  • Dexterity: Cannot be empty, must be a positive number equal to or less than 30.
  • Constitution: Cannot be empty, must be a positive number equal to or less than 30.
  • Intelligence: Cannot be empty, must be a positive number equal to or less than 30.
  • Wisdom: Cannot be empty, must be a positive number equal to or less than 30.
  • Charisma: Cannot be empty, must be a positive number equal to or less than 30.
  • Hitpoints: Cannot be empty.
  • Character Icon: If supplied, it must be a valid image publically available from another site. Otherwise it defaults to your Google profile picture.
