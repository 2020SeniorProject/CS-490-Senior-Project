How to Make a Room

This document details how to make a room. In order to open a room, you need a room! Likewise, you also need to be logged in in order to make a room.

  1. From the home screen, click the "Room" tab on the navigation bar at the top.
  2. You should see a list of all your rooms. Click the green "Create a Character!" button at the top of the page
  3. You should a battle map with a form in the upper right corner. Fill out the form and click on "Add Room!". If your form has errors, fix the indicated issues, then resubmit."*
  4. Upon submitting, you should be brought back to the main room page. You will see your room in your list of rooms!

*The form has the following rules

  • Room Name: The name can only be up to 42 characters long. You must enter something for the name.
  • DM Notes: This is optional. No restrictions on this.
  • Map URL: it must link to an image hosted on a publically available address. The address must end with .jpg, .jpeg, .webp, .gif, or .png.
