How to Change Your Username

This document details how to change your usename. You must be logged in to have a username.

  1. From the home screen, click the "Settings" tab on the navigation bar at the top.
  2. You should see a a listing of your email and your username.
  3. In the username section of the page, type your new username in the provide form and click the "Update" button.*
  4. Your username is now updated!

*The username must be unique across all users.
