How to Add an NPC to the Battlemap

This document details how to add an NPC to the battlemap. You need to have opened a room as a Dungeon Master in order to add an NPC to the battlemap.

  1. Upon opening a room, you will see the battlemap screen.
  2. Click the button labeled "Add NPC" located in the bottom right corner of the screen.
  3. You've added an NPC to the battlemap! You should see an icon appear in the top left corner of the battlemap.
